One of the clearest ideas in the public project work is that the web has accumulated a lot of noise.

Landing pages often feel like they are trying to do everything at once:

- persuade
- posture
- decorate
- hedge against indecision

The result is often clutter that asks the user to work harder, not interfaces that help the user move cleanly.

## The alternative

A better landing page behaves more like a functional tool. It reduces rather than bloats. It is deliberate about what deserves emphasis and what should disappear.

That is the design stance behind the current site work as well.
