This repository is the backbone of the public website.

## What it is for

The site is meant to hold more than a single landing page. It needs room for:

- projects
- writing
- design investigations
- ongoing notes that may later become something more formal

## Current direction

The current rebuild moves the site from a one-file HTML page into Astro with:

- reusable layout and navigation
- a writing section
- a projects section
- markdown-backed content entries
- GitHub Pages deployment through Actions

That is the minimum structure needed to keep publishing without rebuilding the site every time a new page appears.
