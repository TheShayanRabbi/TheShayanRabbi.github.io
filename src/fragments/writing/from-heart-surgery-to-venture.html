The shortest public version of the transition is still the cleanest one:

I used to do heart surgery. Now I do venture.

The line works because it is direct. It does not pretend the path is linear, and it does not over-explain the shift before the work has a chance to speak for itself.

Why it belongs on the site

This website is not only a portfolio. It is a place to make the transition legible through projects, notes, and design investigations.

The biography matters, but only insofar as it helps explain the work that follows.
